About Us

  • Vision
  • We are committed by 2023 to be able to regionally adopt, cure and help all the stray dogs. We are also looking forward to give shelters to cats, cows and other homeless animals just to feed them and help them live peacefully. At the same time, our team has decided to expand its branches in order to sell more animal care products thereby raising more funds for the animals.
  • Mission
  • Mission of this pet care NGO is to help the animals to live who want to live, and help adopters to adopt them who want to do the same. Our mission is not to see any animal being abused in the streets or die of hunger. This organization is totally non-profitable as our team do it just for raising the funds for stray animals.

About our History

We started off this initiative from our first Doggo named 'Ketchup' who used to live in the streets of this city. Some local guys used to feed him some milk and bread while going to office. One day some random kids were hitting poor Ketchup and she hid behind someone's car. That guy called us if we can give him our parking space of old house. We started off as a rental to him but then the family started growing and we are now part of that family.

Although we love being around so many puppies, but we always feel happy when one gets a real family.
